Budgeting

Budgeting is the cornerstone of financial responsibility. Track your income and allocate it wisely to needs, wants, and savings. The 50/30/20 rule is a great start: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings. Start small, stay consistent, and track your progress to build discipline.
